Our Founder

Meet Adeeb Sekander

Adeeb Sekander is a decorated U.S. Army veteran who completed two tours of duty overseas before channeling his military discipline and operational expertise into the private security sector. With a career spanning over 18 years across federal law enforcement support, corporate risk management, and personal protection, Adeeb founded Sekander Security Group with a single mission: to deliver military-grade protection with civilian-grade professionalism.

Adeeb holds the Certified Protection Professional (CPP) designation from ASIS International — the gold standard in the private security industry — along with advanced training in counter-surveillance, emergency medical response, and executive protection from the Executive Security International (ESI) program. He has personally led security operations for high-profile corporate conferences, private estates, government contractors, and international delegations throughout the Washington DC Metro area.

"Every client deserves to feel safe. That's not a marketing line — it's a personal commitment I make before every engagement."
